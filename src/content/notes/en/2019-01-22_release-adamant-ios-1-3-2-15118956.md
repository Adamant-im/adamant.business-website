This release introduces onboarding screens to improve the first-run experience for new users of the ADAMANT iOS app.

The update also improves handling of dummy accounts that do not yet have a public key, meaning they have not been initialised on the network. Users can send funds to such accounts, though messaging remains unavailable until the account is initialised. When sending tokens to an uninitialised or not-found account, the app now displays an alert with a confirmation prompt before proceeding.
