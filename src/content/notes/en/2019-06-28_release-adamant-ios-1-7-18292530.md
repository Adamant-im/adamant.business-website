This release of ADAMANT for iOS introduces fancy push notifications that display rich content directly from the lock screen and notification center. The update adds three notification extensions that enhance how incoming transfers and messages are previewed without opening the app.

The NotificationServiceExtension handles the processing and enrichment of incoming push payloads, allowing media attachments and styled content to be added before delivery. The TransferContentExtension renders a compact preview of incoming ADM and token transfers, showing relevant transaction details at a glance. The MessageContentExtension provides an inline preview of chat messages so users can read incoming text without unlocking the device.

Together these extensions improve the day-to-day notification experience by making push alerts more informative and actionable while maintaining the privacy expectations of ADAMANT users.
