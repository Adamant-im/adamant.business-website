Many token owners want active trading for their asset, but on mid-sized exchanges even well-known tokens often see little real volume. This creates a feedback problem: traders tend to engage with tokens that already show activity, leaving smaller projects stuck.

The ADAMANT Trading and Market Making bot addresses this gap. It is open-source, free software that runs on a server or virtual machine and is managed directly through an ADAMANT Messenger chat. The bot supports automated trading on crypto exchanges, wash trading to simulate volume, and dynamic order book construction that mimics organic market depth.

To use it for your own token, install and configure the bot on a host of your choosing, then operate it through the Messenger interface.
