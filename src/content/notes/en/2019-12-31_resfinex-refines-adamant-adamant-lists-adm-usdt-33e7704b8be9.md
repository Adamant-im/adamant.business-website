ADAMANT has entered a partnership with Resfinex, a crypto exchange built from scratch with a custom matching engine rather than a standardized one. Resfinex requires only email confirmation for signup and does not enforce KYC procedures. The platform focuses on Ethereum and ERC-20 tokens paired with USDT, is fully mobile-friendly, and prioritizes user safety and discretion. The exchange is operated by a team based in the British Virgin Islands.

![Resfinex refines ADAMANT — ADAMANT lists ADM/USDT](/images/engineering-notes/medium/33e7704b8be9/002-1-qo-atak9jpw9kevtt8jbgq-png.webp)

![Resfinex refines ADAMANT — ADAMANT lists ADM/USDT](/images/engineering-notes/medium/33e7704b8be9/003-1-wzjvzpttwmwlyo9c17j8bg-png.webp)

The partnership covers several technical integrations. ADAMANT users will be able to store and send Resfinex Tokens (RES) directly in-chat; RES reduces exchange fees by 50% on Resfinex. A demo build of the RES integration for ADAMANT is already available for testing. Over time, RES support will also be added to ADAMANT's built-in currency exchangers, helping extend the Resfinex ecosystem into the messenger.

![Resfinex refines ADAMANT — ADAMANT lists ADM/USDT](/images/engineering-notes/medium/33e7704b8be9/004-1-bas9r9eklnbyqz8obihr1w-png.webp)

ADM is now tradable against USDT on Resfinex. Beyond token support, the partnership explores applying ADAMANT's decentralized messaging to exchange operations. Potential integrations include two-factor authentication via ADAMANT, exchange account creation without tied email addresses, a notification system through the messenger, and user support conducted through ADAMANT accounts. Joint bounty campaigns are also under consideration.
