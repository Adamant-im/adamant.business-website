### Exchange Dogecoin in ADAMANT chats

![Exchange Dogecoin in chats](/images/engineering-notes/medium/16c94a625517/002-0-9mopouvx-g3rjexs.webp)

ADAMANT Exchanger v2.3.0 introduces Dogecoin (DOGE) support, allowing users to exchange DOGE directly within anonymous ADAMANT chats. The updated exchanger now supports swaps between DOGE, BTC, ETH, DASH, ADM, BNB, USDS, and RES.

The release is available on the ADAMANT GitHub repository.
