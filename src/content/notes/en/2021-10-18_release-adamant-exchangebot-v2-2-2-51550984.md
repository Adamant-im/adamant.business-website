This release expands cryptocurrency support in ADAMANT Exchangebot by adding Bitcoin (BTC), Stably Dollar (USDS) as an ERC20 token, and Binance Coin (BNB) as an ERC20 token. These additions give users more flexibility when trading through the bot.

### Breaking changes

Support for BZ coin has been removed in this release. Users who were previously trading BZ coin through the bot will need to update their configurations accordingly.
