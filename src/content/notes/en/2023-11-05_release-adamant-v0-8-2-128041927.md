This release introduces a `confirmations` field on the `/api/chatrooms/U*` endpoint, providing callers with additional context about the confirmation status of relevant transactions. This enhancement makes it easier for clients consuming the chatroom API to determine how deeply a transaction has been confirmed on the blockchain without needing a separate lookup.
