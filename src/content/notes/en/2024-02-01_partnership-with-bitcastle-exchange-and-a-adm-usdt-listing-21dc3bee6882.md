ADAMANT has partnered with bitcastle exchange, a global cryptocurrency trading platform launched in August 2022. The collaboration centers on integrating the ADM blockchain into bitcastle's trading platform, including an ADM/USDT listing whose date is forthcoming.

Beyond the listing, the partnership extends into several technical areas. bitcastle's API will be implemented in the open-source [TradeBot](https://github.com/Adamant-im/admant-tradebot) and [Coinoptimus](https://github.com/Adamant-im/adamant-coinoptimus), expanding the toolset available to crypto projects and traders. There is also potential for [ADM's Blockchain 2FA](https://blockchain2fa.io/) to be integrated into bitcastle's security system as an alternative to Google Authenticator, and for bitcastle's native CASTLE token to be incorporated into ADAMANT applications, giving token holders access to a non-custodial wallet with in-chat transfers.

bitcastle offers 0% trading fees on major pairs and targets both experienced and beginner traders, with a notably strong Japanese community.
