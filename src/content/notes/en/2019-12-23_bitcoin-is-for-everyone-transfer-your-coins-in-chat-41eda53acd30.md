ADAMANT has integrated Bitcoin into ADAMANT Messenger, enabling in-chat BTC transfers alongside previously supported coins such as ETH, DOGE, and DASH. The integration follows ADAMANT's approach of keeping cryptocurrency handling simple: each user who installs the messenger gets an integrated crypto wallet without needing to understand Bitcoin address mechanics, and sending or receiving coins works as straightforwardly as sharing an image or document in chat.

Account creation requires no email address or phone number. Users receive an account with several integrated crypto wallets immediately upon installation, which lowers the barrier for newcomers who might otherwise find nodes, hardware wallets, and dedicated apps complicated or time-consuming.

![Bitcoin is for everyone: transfer your coins in-chat](/images/engineering-notes/medium/41eda53acd30/002-0-hcqccp8jyyx9dy2k.webp)

Private keys are never transmitted to the network. All transactions are signed locally and then broadcast to a separate node on the decentralized Bitcoin network, so users retain full control over their funds. Private key export is available in the iOS app, with support for other ADAMANT platforms planned.
