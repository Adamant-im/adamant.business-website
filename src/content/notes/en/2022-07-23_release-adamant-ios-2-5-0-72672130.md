This release of ADAMANT iOS introduces the Chatrooms API, enabling more flexible integration with chat functionality. Crypto rate fetching has been optimized to retrieve pricing data more quickly, improving overall responsiveness within the app.

General performance has been improved across the board, and the build now includes improvements for MacBook M1 hardware. Several bugfixes are also included in this release.
