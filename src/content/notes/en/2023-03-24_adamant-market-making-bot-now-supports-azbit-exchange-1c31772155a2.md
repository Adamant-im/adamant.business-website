The ADAMANT TradeBot is an open-source, self-hosted automated trading solution designed for market-making and liquidity provision. By integrating support for the Azbit exchange, the tool offers crypto project owners and exchanges expanded opportunities to manage assets effectively.

Market making is a crucial aspect of the cryptocurrency ecosystem. The ADAMANT tradebot helps maintain consistent buy and sell orders in the order book, which improves liquidity and ensures a steady flow of transactions. By placing orders around the current market price, the bot reduces price fluctuations and fosters a stable trading environment. This continuous activity increases overall trading volume, potentially boosting exchange rankings and project visibility. Furthermore, consistent liquidity discourages sudden large orders that could lead to extreme price swings, thereby reducing price manipulation and enhancing market perception.

The latest release, version 5.4.0, introduces support for the Azbit exchange and adds a new `getOrderDetails()` method. This update also removes support for the Resfinex exchange and includes various dependency updates. The source code is available on the ADAMANT GitHub repository.
