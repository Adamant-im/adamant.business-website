The ADAMANT team has released the official ADAMANT Messenger application for iOS, now available for download on the Apple App Store. To install the application, search for "ADAMANT Messenger" in the App Store on your iPhone.

The iOS application has undergone a complete redesign, introducing new features not previously available in earlier builds. The initial release is tagged as version 1.0.0 on the ADAMANT GitHub repository.
