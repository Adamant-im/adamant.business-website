The ADM cryptocurrency, developed by the ADAMANT community, is set to debut on the KoinBX exchange (formerly Koinbazar), a leading Indian cryptocurrency trading platform. The listing aims to increase the accessibility and liquidity of ADM. In celebration, ADAMANT is launching an airdrop campaign offering the community a chance to earn ADM tokens at no cost. The listing date is 25th June at 12:00 UTC, with the airdrop campaign running from 25th June through 2nd July.

### Overview of ADM

ADM is the native cryptocurrency of the [ADAMANT platform](https://adamant.im/), a decentralized, secure, and anonymous messaging and payments ecosystem. ADAMANT emphasizes privacy and security, allowing users to communicate and transact without compromising personal information. The platform's robust encryption protocols and decentralized architecture make it a standout choice for those prioritizing confidentiality in their digital interactions.

![ADM listing on KoinBX and airdrop campaign](/images/engineering-notes/medium/be3dec3e10b0/002-0-acoeb9t5u7jgqycy.webp)

### Airdrop Campaign

To celebrate the ADM listing, ADAMANT is hosting an airdrop campaign with a total of 10,000 ADM tokens (~300 USDT) distributed to participants. Ten random winners will receive 500 ADM each (~15 USDT), and 50 random winners will receive 100 ADM each (~3 USDT). Eligibility requires a KoinBX account, an ADM deposit address, and completion of specified social tasks detailed on [ADAMANT's X post](https://x.com/adamant_im/status/1805542593477939430). Airdrop tokens will be distributed within two days after the campaign ends, with the ADAMANT team sending rewards directly to deposit addresses on KoinBX.

### Participating in the Ecosystem

The airdrop provides an opportunity to earn ADM tokens and engage with the growing ADAMANT and KoinBX communities. Recipients can use their ADM tokens to explore the features of the private messaging platform. ADAMANT apps are available at [https://adamant.im/#adm-apps](https://adamant.im/#adm-apps).
