ADAMANT messenger now supports in-chat Ether transfers, allowing any user to send and receive payments for goods and services directly within a conversation. This feature is equally suited for personal transfers between friends and family who need a secure way to move funds online.

Users who have purchased over 100 ADM can claim a small amount of ETH to test the transfer feature. To do so, log in to the ADAMANT messenger and send a message to account **U7177493685446622569** explaining how you learned about ADAMANT. After contacting this account, you will receive 0.01 ETH to test in-chat transactions.

This testing program gives holders an opportunity to verify the transfer workflow end-to-end before relying on it for larger payments.
