Resfinex Token (RES) reduces trading fees by 50% on the Resfinex exchange. ADAMANT extends the token's utility beyond the exchange itself: users can store and send RES directly within chats in the ADAMANT Messenger.

RES is also supported in ADAMANT exchangers, which are open for anyone to create and operate on the ADAMANT platform. This means RES can be exchanged against other supported cryptocurrencies through decentralized exchanger nodes running on the ADAMANT network.

ADAMANT and Resfinex plan to launch shared bounty campaigns in the near future.
