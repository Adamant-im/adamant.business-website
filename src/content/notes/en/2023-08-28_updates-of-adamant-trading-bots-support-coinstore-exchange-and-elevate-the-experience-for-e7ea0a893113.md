The ADAMANT open-source project has updated its Market-Making and CoinOptimus trading bots to support the Coinstore exchange. The Market-Making bot reaches version 5.6.0, while CoinOptimus is updated to version 1.5.0.

The Market-Making bot is designed for crypto project owners to maintain liquidity and consistent spreads. It sets price ranges to balance buy and sell orders, generating a dynamic order book that mimics live trading conditions.

![Updates of ADAMANT Trading bots support Coinstore exchange and Elevate the Experience for Crypto…](/images/engineering-notes/medium/e7ea0a893113/002-1-ji7ldfgywe0whe5fgx-qag-gif.webp)

CoinOptimus targets occasional traders looking to automate their strategies and remove emotional decision-making. As a self-hosted tool, it ensures users retain full control over their keys and trading operations. Its modular structure allows tailored support for different exchanges and strategies. The bot employs an optimal ladder or grid trade strategy, aiming to buy at lower prices and sell at higher prices to secure profit margins.

By adding Coinstore exchange support, both bots offer traders expanded flexibility and additional options for executing automated trading strategies.
