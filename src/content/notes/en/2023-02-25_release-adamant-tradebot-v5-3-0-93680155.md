This release of ADAMANT Tradebot enhances the /balances command by displaying additional information, giving users more detailed insight into their exchange balances. The update also includes various bug fixes and dependency updates to maintain stability and security.

### Breaking changes

Support for the Coindeal exchange has been removed in this version. Users who were relying on Coindeal will need to update their configurations accordingly.
