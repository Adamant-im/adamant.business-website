After almost a year of full-scale, rapid development, the ADAMANT team has completed a large number of challenging tasks across the platform.

The progress reflects the collective effort of users and supporters who share the project's values around private, decentralized communication. Opposing the conventional models of messengers provides a strong foundation for building the ADAMANT platform and its capabilities.

Development of the ADAMANT network source code continues actively. Messenger functions are expanding, and the visual appearance across all platforms is improving, including minor details that matter to users. Each update to the ADAMANT Messenger reflects ongoing work.

The belief in an idea from a small group of individuals has always been able to change the world. Thank you to everyone who continues to support the project.
