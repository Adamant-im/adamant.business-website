This release adds support for the Azbit exchange and introduces a new `getOrderDetails()` method. Dependencies have also been updated to their latest versions.

### Breaking changes

Support for the Resfinex exchange has been removed in this release. Users who relied on Resfinex will need to update their configurations accordingly.
