This release of ADAMANT for iOS introduces an End User License Agreement and removes the in-app token purchase option to comply with Apple's policies for anonymous messengers. The app has been updated for iOS 13, with additional adjustments to meet Apple's App Store requirements.

Chat improvements include saving chat position, scrolling to the first unread message, and a new "scroll to bottom" button on the chat screen. The update also adds duplication checking for third-party transactions and includes multiple bug fixes.
