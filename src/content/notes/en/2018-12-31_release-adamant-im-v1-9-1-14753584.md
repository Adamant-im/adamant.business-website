This maintenance release of ADAMANT Messenger addresses several issues reported by users. The camera functionality has been fixed for compatibility with Chrome 71, resolving a regression that affected in-app photo capture. Emoji rendering has been corrected to display properly across conversations and message inputs. Email link handling has also been improved, ensuring that email addresses are detected and opened correctly.

In addition to these targeted fixes, the release includes a number of other minor corrections and stability improvements throughout the application.
