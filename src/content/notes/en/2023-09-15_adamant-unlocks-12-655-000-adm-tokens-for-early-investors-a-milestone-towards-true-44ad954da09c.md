### ADAMANT Unlocks 12,655,000 ADM Tokens for Early Investors

The ADAMANT project has released 12,655,000 ADM tokens to early investors who have supported the project since 2017. This distribution marks the completion of a long-held commitment to those backers and represents a meaningful step toward full project decentralization.

Early supporters played a central role in the development of the ADAMANT Messenger, contributing both capital and patience throughout the project's multi-year build-out. With this unlock, their originally purchased tokens are now available for use on the network.

All ADM tokens, including those previously locked by the ADAMANT team, are now freely circulating. This eliminates remaining vesting restrictions and aligns the token model with the project's goal of a fair, decentralized ecosystem where no single party retains privileged control over supply.

The increased circulating supply also broadens access for new participants. Crypto users can now acquire ADM more readily and take part in network operations by running a delegate node, establishing a forging pool, or voting for existing delegates. These mechanisms form the core of ADAMANT's on-chain governance, allowing token holders to influence the direction and security of the network directly.

> [](https://twitter.com/adamant_im/status/1693157793975488614)

This milestone effectively closes the initial token allocation phase. With the full supply now in circulation, the project's decentralization depends on the ongoing participation of holders, delegates, and node operators rather than on controlled releases from a team treasury.
