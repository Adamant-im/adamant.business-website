The 2.1 release of ADAMANT Messenger for iOS focuses on stability improvements and bug fixes. The application no longer crashes during account login and continues to operate smoothly even if the Ethereum node is temporarily unavailable. 

Several issues affecting Dash and Doge have been addressed. Transaction history for Dash is now accurate, translations display correct information, and the update introduces a new block explorer for Dash. Additionally, underlying libraries have been updated to enhance overall application stability.
