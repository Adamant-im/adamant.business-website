ADAMANT Messenger v3.2.0 introduces fiat rate estimates displayed at market rate. Users can select their preferred currency in Settings.

The release also updates dependencies and includes bug fixes. The full changelog is available in the [v3.2.0 release notes](https://github.com/Adamant-im/adamant-im/releases/tag/v3.2.0). The ADAMANT source repository is hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/Adamant-im/adamant-im) for those who wish to build the application themselves.
