The ADAMANT team is currently developing the blockchain messaging platform and an update that includes a push notifications service. ADAMANT Messenger is designed as a complete blockchain-based communication platform rather than just a digital messenger.

The blockchain in ADAMANT functions as a distributed storage of connected transactions protected by cryptographic encryption, creating immutability over time. Access to this storage is open to the ADAMANT Services system, which is implemented by independent developers to give users additional options.

![ADAMANT is working on Blockchain messaging platform and Push notifications Service](/images/engineering-notes/medium/765972cce50e/002-0-qyqxrmcz3elfkx-o.webp)

These services connect to the blockchain, and user apps connect to a service. The services vary in scope; for example, ADAMANT developers have implemented an instant notification service (Push Notifications) available at [github.com/Adamant-im/AdamantNotificationService](https://github.com/Adamant-im/AdamantNotificationService). This service provides convenience and also saves battery power.

The team is currently working on connecting the push notification service to the iOS application, with plans to extend it to other platforms afterward.
