ADAMANT InfoServices has been updated to v3.5.0, providing a combined fiat and cryptocurrency rates API. The service aggregates data from several upstream sources including MOEX, Currency-Api, ExchangeRates.host, CoinMarketCap, CryptoCompare, and CoinGecko, then cross-references the results to flag discrepancies and improve reliability.

Because it runs on your own server, the service keeps historical rate data under your control and remains free for any use. The project is open source, hosted under the ADAMANT organization on GitHub.

API documentation is available in the [InfoServices API wiki](https://github.com/Adamant-im/adamant-currencyinfo-services/wiki/InfoServices-API-documentation).
