The latest iOS release of ADAMANT Messenger addresses Apple's policy requirements for cryptocurrency-related and anonymous messaging apps, while also adding several chat usability improvements.

On the compliance side, the Welcome screen now includes the End User License Agreement, the age rating has been set to 17+, and the in-app token purchase option has been removed. Links to cryptocurrency exchanges have also been dropped from the application.

The update introduces chat navigation features that make long conversations easier to manage. The app now saves your scroll position when you leave a chat, scrolls automatically to the first unread message when you return, and provides an additional button to jump to the bottom of the chat screen.

Other changes include a duplication check for third-party transactions, fixes for iOS 13 compatibility, and multiple general bug fixes.

Install or update ADAMANT Messenger in the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/adamant-messenger/id1341473829).
