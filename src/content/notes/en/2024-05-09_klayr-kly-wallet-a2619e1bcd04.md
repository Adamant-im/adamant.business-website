Following the Lisk L1 to L2 migration, the Klayr team continues developing an L1 network based on Lisk. ADAMANT has historically supported all major Lisk migrations from version 1 through version 4, but the shift to an Ethereum-based L2 represents a strategic realignment that ADAMANT has chosen not to follow. Instead, ADAMANT will support Klayr (KLY) as a Lisk L1 successor, enabling in-chat KLY transfers.

![Klayr KLY Wallet](/images/engineering-notes/medium/a2619e1bcd04/002-0-vazvb7jsoue-oofz.webp)

ADAMANT Messenger is a non-custodial, open-source wallet and secure messaging platform available for Web, Tor, iOS, Android, Windows, Linux, and macOS. Users retain complete control over their private keys, and accounts can be accessed across multiple devices.

### KLY Wallet Features

At launch, the ADAMANT Wallet will offer holding KLY, sending and receiving transactions directly to a KLY wallet, sending and receiving KLY in chats, exporting Klayr private keys, and transaction history. As Klayr develops its L1 blockchain further, cryptofoundry will consider adding support for Klayr-based tokens, sidechains, and decentralized features such as DeFi or DEX.

### Development Status

Work on KLY support in ADAMANT is already underway. Technical details are available in the [Klayr documentation](https://docs.klayr.xyz) and on [GitHub](https://github.com/orgs/klayrHQ/repositories).
