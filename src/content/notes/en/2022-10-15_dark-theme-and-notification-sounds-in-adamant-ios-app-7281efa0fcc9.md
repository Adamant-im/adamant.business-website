ADAMANT iOS app version 2.7.0 introduces a dark theme, custom notification sounds, chat pre-loading, and displays the ERC20 network for tokens. The dark theme activates automatically based on the OS appearance setting, requiring iOS 13 or later.

![Dark theme and notification sounds in ADAMANT iOS app](/images/engineering-notes/medium/7281efa0fcc9/002-0-dnvwyqzo3m07m35o.webp)

To configure a notification sound, users must first set up a PIN to sign in to the app. Once a PIN is established, the notification sound can be selected via the Notifications menu. Face ID can also be optionally enabled in the Security section of the home screen.

![Dark theme and notification sounds in ADAMANT iOS app](/images/engineering-notes/medium/7281efa0fcc9/003-0-nllz9ighkkepn7-2.webp)

In addition to these features, this version pre-loads recent chats immediately after an account login to improve loading speeds. The update also addresses several stability issues, including crashes that occurred when copying messages or scanning QR codes with the camera, alongside general bug fixes and compatibility improvements for MacBook M1 devices.
