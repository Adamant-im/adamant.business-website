Coinstore, a cryptocurrency exchange based in Singapore, has listed the ADM token, which powers the ADAMANT Messenger platform. The ADM/USDT trading pair provides increased accessibility and liquidity for the ADAMANT ecosystem.

![Coinstore Lists ADM Token (ADAMANT Messenger) with ADM/USDT Trading Pair](/images/engineering-notes/medium/493fd6aa1d98/002-1-asichu0-dwbt5pn-2qdpw-png.webp)

Coinstore began accepting ADM deposits on August 14, 2023, at 10:00 UTC. Trading for the ADM/USDT pair commenced on August 16, 2023, at 10:00 UTC, followed by the activation of withdrawals on August 17, 2023, at 10:00 UTC.

Prior to this listing, ADM was already available on platforms such as StakeCube and Azbit. These listings support the ADAMANT infrastructure by expanding the token's availability to a broader user base. ADAMANT Messenger operates on a decentralized blockchain infrastructure, prioritizing user privacy and data autonomy without central authority oversight. The ADM token facilitates network operations and various ecosystem functionalities.

![Coinstore Lists ADM Token (ADAMANT Messenger) with ADM/USDT Trading Pair](/images/engineering-notes/medium/493fd6aa1d98/003-1-xtguv0nehmquuugifv56ia-png.webp)

The addition of Coinstore further strengthens ADAMANT's independence and resilience, offering users enhanced liquidity and wider adoption avenues for the ADM token.
