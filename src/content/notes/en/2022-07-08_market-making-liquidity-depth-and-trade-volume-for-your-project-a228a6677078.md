The ADAMANT Trade & Market-making bot is a free, open-source application designed to run trades on cryptocurrency exchanges. It generates trade volume, maintains spread and liquidity, sets price ranges, and builds a dynamic, live-like order book. Version 5.0.0 is currently available.

The bot is installed directly on a server or VPS and can initially fill and dynamically build order books. It supports placing buy and sell limit or market orders and offers three market-making policies: spread, orderbook, and optimal. Users can maintain spread and liquidity, enforce price ranges, and arbitrage token prices across other trading pairs or exchanges. Operations are managed via commands sent through ADAMANT Messenger.
