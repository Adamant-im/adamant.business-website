The cryptocurrency industry's growth depends on shared efforts across projects rather than isolated successes. ADAMANT has established a partnership with CoinDeal Global following a community-driven vote on the exchange.

ADAMANT Messenger won the 19th vote on CoinDeal, which led the exchange to integrate ADM wallets and list an ADM/BTC trading pair. ADAMANT and CoinDeal plan joint marketing and bounty campaigns, and are exploring additional areas of cooperation.

![Partnership with CoinDeal Global and ADM/BTC listing](/images/engineering-notes/medium/71dea0455b5d/002-1-irkkoww-naa-einpiio6sg-png.webp)

### About CoinDeal Global

CoinDeal is a cryptocurrency exchange and OTC platform operating in Europe, the USA, and globally. It holds licenses from FinCEN (USA), VQF (Switzerland), and MFSA (Malta). The exchange was noted in The TIE report as the second-best world exchange by "Expected and Reported Volume" among 100 platforms.

The exchange supports fiat trading alongside crypto, with deposits, withdrawals, and trading available for EUR, USD, GBP, PLN, RUB, CHF, and KRW. CoinDeal also offers a CDL token used for fee reductions, premium accounts, and voting on new cryptocurrency listings. Additionally, the exchange provides a bank card that allows payments with crypto and ATM cash withdrawals in local currencies. Both iOS and Android apps are available.
