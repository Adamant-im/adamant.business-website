This release of ADAMANT for iOS introduces Bitcoin wallet functionality, allowing users to manage Bitcoin alongside existing blockchain assets. In-chat transfers for Bitcoin are now supported, enabling users to send Bitcoin directly within conversations.

Additionally, several bugs affecting MacBook M1 devices have been resolved, along with other general improvements and bug fixes throughout the application.
