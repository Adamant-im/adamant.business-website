ADAMANT Messenger v2.8.0 brings lower transaction fees for Ethereum and ERC-20 transfers, continuing the project's effort to make its built-in crypto wallet more cost-effective for everyday use. The update also refreshes the Buy & Sell tokens dialog for a cleaner trading experience.

On the bug-fix front, the release resolves a clipboard issue that had prevented iOS users from using Copy to clipboard buttons — a seemingly small but important fix for mobile wallet usability. Additionally, an Atomars exchange link has been added to the in-app options for token trading.

Full release notes are available in the [ADAMANT Messenger v2.8.0 release](https://github.com/Adamant-im/adamant-im/releases/tag/v2.8.0) on GitHub.
