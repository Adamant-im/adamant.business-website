ADAMANT Exchanger v2.4.4 adds Lisk (LSK) support, enabling in-chat exchanges between LSK and the full set of supported coins: DOGE, BTC, ETH, DASH, ADM, BNB, USDS, and LSK. All trades take place directly within anonymous ADAMANT chat.

This release also introduces a fixed fee option for coins, giving operators more control over exchange pricing.
