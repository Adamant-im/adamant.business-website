This is the initial release of ADAMANT Tradebot, a crypto trading and market making bot. The bot is managed through commands sent via ADAMANT Messenger, making it accessible and easy to control. It is designed to be straightforward to install and configure, and is free and open source.

Exchange support begins with IDCM, with additional exchanges in progress. The bot is capable of filling order books and performing market making operations. It also stores and displays statistics to help users track performance.
