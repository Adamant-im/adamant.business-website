ADM is now listed on Azbit, a centralized cryptocurrency exchange based in Europe, with an ADM/USDT trading pair available to all platform users.

ADM is the utility token powering the ADAMANT decentralized messenger, a blockchain-based platform providing end-to-end encrypted and anonymous messaging. The token supports the network's infrastructure and independence, enabling faster transaction times and lower fees compared to traditional alternatives.

Within the ADAMANT ecosystem, ADM serves several functions. It is used to pay fees for secure and anonymous messaging, and it secures the ADAMANT blockchain itself — a decentralized communication network. Token holders can run a forging delegate or join a forging pool to participate in consensus. ADM also enables access to ADAMANT bots and services, including a crypto exchanger, market making and trading bot, and the Adelina AI assistant. Additionally, ADM holders can participate in project governance through voting.

The Azbit listing increases ADM's accessibility and liquidity, supporting broader adoption of ADAMANT's decentralized communication infrastructure.
