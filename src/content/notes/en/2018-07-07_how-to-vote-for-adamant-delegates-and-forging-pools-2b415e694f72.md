ADAMANT uses delegated Proof of Stake (dPoS) as its blockchain consensus protocol. Delegates ranked in the top 101 active spots forge blocks and earn transaction fees. A forging pool is itself a delegate, so the same voting process applies whether you are supporting an individual delegate or a pool. You can vote for any delegate, including your own.

### Prerequisites

Any account holding at least 50 ADM can vote. The voting fee is 50 ADM regardless of how many delegates you vote for — up to 30 at a time — and is deducted from your account and forged by current delegates. You can upvote to add support or downvote to withdraw a previous vote.

### Voting via the web wallet

Log in at [msg.adamant.im](https://msg.adamant.im) and navigate to **Settings → Vote for delegates**. This screen lists all registered ADAMANT delegates along with their rank and your current votes.

![How to vote for ADAMANT delegates and forging pools](/images/engineering-notes/medium/2b415e694f72/002-1-zrnvu6rsipsvzlhrc0w5aa-png.webp)

The **Name** column shows the registered delegate name and **Rank** shows its position; only the top 101 delegates forge blocks. Clicking a delegate name expands additional information to help you decide whether to support it.

Use the thumbs-up and thumbs-down icons to upvote or downvote delegates, then click **Review voting** to confirm your selections and **Vote** to apply the changes.

![How to vote for ADAMANT delegates and forging pools](/images/engineering-notes/medium/2b415e694f72/003-1-topby8sxxwodlqzftbiwjg-png.webp)

### Voting via Console

Voting can also be performed through the [ADAMANT Console](https://github.com/Adamant-im/adamant-console/wiki/Available-Commands#vote). This option is intended for advanced users and is not recommended for regular wallet users.

For further background, see the ADAMANT documentation on [forging](https://medium.com/adamant-im/earning-money-on-adm-forging-4c7b6eb15516) and on [becoming a delegate](https://medium.com/adamant-im/how-to-become-an-adamant-delegate-745f01d032f).
