Global cryptocurrency exchange BigONE has opened the ADM/USDT trading pair, providing increased exposure for the ADM coin. Deposits became available on March 8, with trading commencing on March 11. Founded in 2017 and registered in the Seychelles, BigONE operates across multiple countries and offers spot, leverage, futures, wealth management, and PoS mining pools.

![BigONE lists ADAMANT Messenger (ADM)](/images/engineering-notes/medium/52589b8383ee/002-0-b8popf5lbqojpfl1.webp)

BigONE emphasizes security through two-step verification, cold and hot wallet separation, multiple signatures, and real-time asset monitoring. The platform maintains 100% asset reserves, allowing users to track their assets at any time. It supports a wide range of blockchain assets, including BTC and ADM, and provides a seamless trading experience across web-based platforms for all operating systems and devices.

![BigONE lists ADAMANT Messenger (ADM)](/images/engineering-notes/medium/52589b8383ee/003-0-3cjo77gxzwzak-dt.webp)

ADM is a delegated Proof-of-Stake (dPoS) utility coin used for message and data transfers within the decentralized ADAMANT Messenger infrastructure. It features a 5-second block time and includes a built-in crypto exchanger for swapping ADM with other cryptocurrencies directly within the messenger apps. Users can set up network delegates to forge ADM or operate forging pools to share rewards among voters.

![BigONE lists ADAMANT Messenger (ADM)](/images/engineering-notes/medium/52589b8383ee/004-0-uej1bhlwf0rl8n6n.webp)

ADAMANT is a decentralized, open-source secure messenger where censorship is technically impossible. It allows anonymous messaging, multi-device account access, and includes a convenient wallet to store and send cryptocurrency. The platform supports apps for Web, Tor, iOS, Android, Windows, and Linux, and features an AI chatbot named Adelina.
