This release fixes starting a new chat with the QR scanner and resolves several chat list issues. You can now tap on URLs in chat to open them in Safari. Remember not to follow links you receive from unknown or untrusted accounts, otherwise your IP address can be compromised.

The chat list now displays a "You: " prefix for sent messages. Old resources have been cleaned up, and the project has been updated to Xcode 9.3 and Swift 4.1.
