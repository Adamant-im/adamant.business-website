The ADAMANT decentralized messenger now includes a Bitcoin wallet with in-chat transfers on iOS. Like the app's other wallets, Bitcoin is non-custodial: the address and private key are derived from your ADAMANT passphrase, meaning only you hold the keys. All wallet keys can be exported from the Export Private Keys screen.

The v2.8.0 release also fixes ETH and ERC20 transactions, resolves bugs affecting MacBook M1 devices, and includes additional improvements and bug fixes.
