ADAMANT Messenger v2.15.0 introduces several wallet and exchange refinements alongside routine maintenance. The release fixes a bug affecting the "send all" function for DOGE, corrects the Lisk explorer to point at mainnet rather than testnet, and updates project dependencies.

On the exchange side, the Buy-Sell ADM screen now links to the built-in exchanger for purchasing ADM, while the Bit-Z option has been removed. ERC20 tokens are now labeled with an ERC20 mark for clearer identification.

The KCS and BZ wallets have been removed from the app. Users who still hold KCS or BZ in their ADAMANT wallet can withdraw those assets using the legacy application at legacy.adamant.im.

The full release details are available on the [ADAMANT GitHub releases page](https://github.com/Adamant-im/adm/releases/tag/v2.15.0).
