ADAMANT Messenger for iOS v2.3.0 adds support for scanning ADAMANT QR codes containing addresses and URI links, bringing the iOS app to parity with the Android and Desktop clients. The implementation follows three ADAMANT Improvement Proposals: AIP-8 and AIP-9 define the URI format for ADAMANT, and AIP-15 specifies the QR code standard for ADM addresses.

To share your ADM address, open the Account tab, tap the address, and select **Generate QR code**. Other users can then scan the resulting code from any ADAMANT client on Android, Desktop, or iOS to initiate a chat or transaction.

This release also fixes a bug where chat names remained static instead of updating correctly.
