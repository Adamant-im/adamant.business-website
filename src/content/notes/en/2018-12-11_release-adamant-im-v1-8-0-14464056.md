This release introduces an ETH transactions list and a redesigned Nodes screen. Icons throughout the app have been updated, and a promotion bar has been added for iOS users.

Several fixes are included in this release. The issue that allowed sending 0 BNB or ETH has been resolved, and a problem with showing email in chat has been fixed.
