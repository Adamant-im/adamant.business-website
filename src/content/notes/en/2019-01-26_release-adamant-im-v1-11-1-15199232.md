This is a minor release with several small fixes and improvements across the ADAMANT platform.

The DOGE wallet now works correctly without requiring a relogin, resolving a frustrating issue for users. Transaction UX for DOGE has also been improved with clearer positive feedback. Additionally, crypto-wallet addresses are now saved as soon as ADM appears on the wallet, ensuring addresses are persisted promptly rather than waiting for a later trigger. Several other small improvements round out this release.
