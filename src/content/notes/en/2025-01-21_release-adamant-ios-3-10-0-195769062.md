ADAMANT Messenger for iOS v3.10.0 is now available, bringing full compatibility with iOS 18. This release focuses on performance and stability improvements, including optimized health checks that contribute to a faster and smoother chat experience.

Several new features have been introduced to improve navigation and media handling, along with UI enhancements aimed at delivering a more user-friendly experience. The update also addresses bug fixes specifically related to iOS 18 compatibility, as well as many smaller improvements and fixes throughout the application.
