ADAMANT Messenger v2.8.0 resolves an issue with copying text to the clipboard on iOS devices, making that functionality reliable again across Apple platforms. The release also decreases transaction fees for Ethereum and ERC20 transfers, reducing the cost of sending and receiving supported assets within the messenger. Additionally, a link to the Atomars exchange has been added for convenient access.
