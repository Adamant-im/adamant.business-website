This release fixes a threading issue in the ADAMANT iOS application. The QR code reader was sometimes called on a background thread, which could lead to unexpected behavior during scanning operations. With this fix, the QR code reader is now consistently invoked on the correct thread, improving stability when users scan wallet addresses or other QR codes within the app.
