Due to China regulations, the Bit-Z exchange will officially stop operations on October 21, 2021.

ADM/USDT trading remains available until October 14, 2021 at 15:00 (UTC+8). ADM withdrawals must be completed by October 21, 2021.

After Bit-Z closes, you can continue to trade ADM on other exchanges and via the in-chat Exchanger within the ADAMANT messenger.
