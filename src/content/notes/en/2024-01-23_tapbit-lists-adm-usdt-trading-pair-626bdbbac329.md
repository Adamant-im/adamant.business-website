Tapbit maintains a USD 40 million insurance fund intended to cover platform-related losses and states that it operates under applicable compliance policies.

ADAMANT is an open-source decentralized messenger independent from governments, corporations, and developers. No party can control, block, or censor accounts. The messenger supports in-chat crypto transfers and is accessible across platforms.

ADM is the native token of the ADAMANT ecosystem. A small ADM fee is required to send messages, which underpins the messenger's decentralization. ADM holders can run a forging delegate or join a forging pool. Within ADAMANT, users can send BTC, ERC-20, Doge, Dash, and Lisk transactions directly in chat, with all such transactions visible in a single interface.
