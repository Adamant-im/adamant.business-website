On July 29, the Atomars cryptocurrency exchange opened ADM/USDT trading. ADAMANT previously won the exchange's community voting round, securing 10,551 votes—the highest tally among participating projects.

Alongside the listing, Atomars and the ADAMANT project plan to launch an ADM token airdrop to introduce users to the capabilities of the ADAMANT decentralized messenger. ADAMANT is an open-source blockchain-based messaging platform that uses its native ADM token for on-chain transactions and in-app transfers.
