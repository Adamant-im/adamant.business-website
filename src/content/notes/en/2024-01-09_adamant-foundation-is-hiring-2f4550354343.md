ADAMANT is expanding its team and seeking developers and a business development manager to advance its decentralized messaging ecosystem. All roles require a strong alignment with privacy values and enthusiasm for the project.

### Business Development Manager
This full-time role focuses on growing the ADM community by working with ambassadors, partners, and clients. Candidates must be fluent in English and skilled in content creation across Medium and X/Twitter, including image and video editing. A solid understanding of blockchain technology, community management, and marketing strategies for blockchain products is essential.

### iOS Developer
The iOS developer will maintain and develop the native ADAMANT iOS client using Swift. The technology stack includes UIKit, SwiftUI, Combine, Swinject, Core Data, GCD, MVVM, VIPER, SnapKit, Git, Socket.io, REST, CocoaPods, and SPM. This is a full-time or part-time position requiring at least 25 hours per week.

### Frontend Developer (Vue/TypeScript)
This role involves developing the ADAMANT Progressive Web Application using Vue. Candidates need deep knowledge of JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, and Vue3, along with experience building scalable web applications, RESTful APIs, and microservices. Familiarity with Git and testing frameworks like Jest and Mocha is required. The position is full-time or part-time with a minimum of 25 hours per week.

### Backend JavaScript Developer (Node.js)
The backend developer will work on the core ADAMANT blockchain node and surrounding services. Candidates must understand blockchain technology, dPoS consensus, transactions, cryptography signatures, and encryption algorithms. Deep knowledge of JavaScript, TypeScript, database systems (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis), Git, and testing frameworks is required. This is a full-time or part-time role with a minimum of 25 hours per week.

### Full-stack JavaScript Developer (Vue/Node.js)
Full-stack developers will contribute to various ADAMANT ecosystem tools, including the Botfactory, Exchanger, Blockchain Explorer, Forging pool, CoinOptimus, and TradeBot. Candidates need expertise in JavaScript, TypeScript, Node.js, Vue3 (or React/AngularJS), databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis), and testing frameworks. This is a full-time or part-time role with a minimum of 25 hours per week.

### Application Process
To apply, send a CV to msg # adamant.im or use the contact page at adamant.im/contact. ADAMANT ignores applications generated by ChatGPT and conducts interviews to verify skills. Candidates should share cryptopunk and privacy values, bring enthusiasm, and be prepared to work hard. Salaries are negotiable but modest.
