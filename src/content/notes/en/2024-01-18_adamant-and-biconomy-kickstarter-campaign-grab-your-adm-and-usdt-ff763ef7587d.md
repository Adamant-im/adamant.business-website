Following cryptofoundry's ongoing cooperation with Biconomy, ADAMANT launched an ADM & USDT airdrop campaign on the Biconomy exchange.

To participate, users must create a Biconomy account and deposit at least 300 USDT, maintaining that balance from 2024-01-20 through 2024-02-04 14:00:00. On February 4th, participants visit the ADM Kickstarter campaign page and commit. Successful commitments are used only for reward calculation; no USDT is frozen. Rewards can be claimed on February 6th.
