A new forging pool called `ahead_pool` has joined the ADAMANT Messenger network, offering a reward distribution model that shares 77% of its forging rewards with pool members. Beyond member payouts, the pool donates 7% of its rewards to the ADAMANT Foundation, the non-profit organization that supports development and growth of the ADAMANT network. This allocation reflects a commitment to both participant incentives and long-term network health.

For technical details and real-time performance statistics, `ahead_pool` provides a dedicated admin board at [http://65.108.159.235:36667](http://65.108.159.235:36667). Members can monitor forging activity, reward distribution, and pool performance directly through this interface.

For a full list of ADAMANT forging pools and instructions on how to vote, refer to the [ADAMANT pools guide](https://news.adamant.im/hodl-list-of-adamant-pools-join-in-and-get-rewards-491a98610f4b).
