The ADAMANT open-source project has released an updated media pack containing a range of branding and promotional materials for community advocates, partners, and press use.

The pack includes promotional materials and eye-catching banners in various sizes suitable for online advertising or website placement. It also contains high-resolution screenshots that highlight the ADAMANT Messenger interface, along with a concise OnePager summarizing key project information at a glance.

For branding needs, the pack provides the classic ADAMANT logo in multiple formats, as well as a distorted variant offering an artistic interpretation. Apparel designs for ADAMANT-branded T-shirts are also included. Additionally, the pack contains video content that demonstrates the messenger in action.
