**Note: This incentive has ended.**

Forging pools strengthen decentralization of the ADAMANT network. To encourage participation, the ADAMANT Foundation offered to compensate the 3000 ADM network fee to anyone who runs a qualifying forging pool.

To qualify, a pool needed to meet the following criteria. The pool operator had to run a forging pool and share its dashboard URL with the Foundation. The pool was required to have at least one vote and share at least 75% of the block rewards with voters. The pool's position in the delegate list did not matter. Operators were also asked to share their pool on social media.

An example dashboard URL format is `http://163.172.183.198:36667/`.
