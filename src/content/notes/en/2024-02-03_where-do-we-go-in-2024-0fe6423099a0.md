ADAMANT has updated its roadmap for 2024, concentrating on messenger features and enhancing ADM exposure.

### Messenger features

While the main goal of private messaging has been achieved, the current focus is implementing "daily features" for convenient messaging. These include transferring files and images, group chats, channels, and push notifications for Android and PWA.

![Where do we go in 2024](/images/engineering-notes/medium/0fe6423099a0/002-0-rjohmkkgpdeqesfj.webp)

ADAMANT will provide a user experience comparable to commonly used messengers, but with blockchain-related advantages and privacy. File and image transfers will utilize decentralized storage such as IPFS — an approach not yet seen in other messenger apps. Group chats and channels will also introduce blockchain-specific features.

### Wallet features

ADAMANT's cryptocurrency wallet is already convenient and secure. The 2024 goal is to make it the most reliable wallet as well. Planned improvements include enhancing coin blockchain infrastructure, implementing health checks for each coin and service, allowing users to add custom coin nodes and disable default ones, and adding support for more tokens.

![Where do we go in 2024](/images/engineering-notes/medium/0fe6423099a0/003-0-xdoj0ola7o9t-c-a.webp)

### Marketing and ADM exposure

In 2023, the ADM coin arrived at many exchanges and received attention from traders. This approach continues in 2024, with multiple marketing campaigns and airdrops aimed at bringing more users to the ADAMANT messaging platform. Specific plans include releasing ADM airdrop software, an ambassador campaign, and additional ADM exchange listings.

![Where do we go in 2024](/images/engineering-notes/medium/0fe6423099a0/004-0-vsesrfydzot28jcw.webp)

### ADAMANT Blockchain and Explorer

The Foundation is preparing several updates in this area, most of which will go unnoticed by regular users but are essential steps toward the project's long-term vision. Details will be shared as development progresses.

### Summary

The community has been developing the decentralized messenger platform since 2017, with everyday progress. As ADAMANT's founder noted, the team is building a messenger without precedent, where even small functions familiar to classic messengers require detailed study and new approaches. After extensive progress in 2023, 2024 is expected to bring even faster growth, concentrating on messenger features and ADM exposure, with further blockchain-specific features planned beyond what is publicly disclosed.
