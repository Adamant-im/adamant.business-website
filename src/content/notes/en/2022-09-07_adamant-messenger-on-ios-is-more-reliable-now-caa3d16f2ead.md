The ADAMANT Messenger iOS app (v2.6.0) now connects to multiple ADAMANT nodes rather than relying on a single endpoint. As long as at least one node remains available, the messenger continues to function. Users can manage the node list themselves, including running their own node and disabling the defaults for improved anonymity.

This update also refreshes the Delegate voting screen with an improved UX. Additional changes include a fix for the German localization along with general bug and crash fixes.

A short video demonstrating the node health check feature is available on [YouTube](https://youtube.com/shorts/V7-adXPad9E).
