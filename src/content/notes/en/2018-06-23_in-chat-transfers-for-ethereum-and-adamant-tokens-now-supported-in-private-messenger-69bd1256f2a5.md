ADAMANT Messenger now supports in-chat Ether transfers, completing a major effort to integrate cryptocurrency transfers into the decentralized messaging platform. Users can send ETH directly within a chat to pay for goods and services or to exchange secure, instant payments with contacts. The feature complements the existing ADM transfer support, with additional token options planned for the future.

To initiate a transfer, a user opens a chat with the intended recipient, clicks the plus icon next to the message input field, and selects either "Send ADM" or "Send ETH." After entering the amount and confirming the transaction, the transfer is complete.

ETH transfers in ADAMANT are secured by the user's own private keys—only the account owner controls the Ether wallet within their ADAMANT account. To use the feature, users top up their ADAMANT ETH wallet by transferring Ether from an external wallet or exchange. The Ethereum wallet address is available on the Wallet tab and can be copied with a tap.

![In-Chat Transfers for Ethereum and ADAMANT Tokens Now Supported in Private Messenger](/images/engineering-notes/medium/69bd1256f2a5/005-0-8iboruozm3te6si.webp)

The combination of private communication and financial transactions makes ADAMANT suitable for sellers who need a discreet platform for coordinating sales with buyers, as well as for individuals seeking secure person-to-person payments without leaving the chat interface.
