ADAMANT chats include an in-chat exchange bot that lets you buy and sell ADM using Bitcoin, Dogecoin, Ethereum, Lisk, Dash, and other supported cryptocurrencies.

To buy ADM, top up your ADAMANT wallet with a regular crypto transfer. For example, you can send LSK from an external exchange such as Binance directly to the ADAMANT Exchange bot within the chat.

The exchange bot charges a fee for its service. Before proceeding, check the current exchange parameters by sending the **/help** command to the bot. Always start with a small test transaction using the **/test** command to verify the exchange rate and flow before committing larger amounts.

To complete an exchange, transfer cryptocurrency to the ADAMANT Exchange bot in the chat. If you are new to the platform, you will first need an ADAMANT account and familiarity with the messenger interface.

If the exchange terms do not suit you, ADAMANT is open source and allows anyone to create their own Exchanger on the platform.
