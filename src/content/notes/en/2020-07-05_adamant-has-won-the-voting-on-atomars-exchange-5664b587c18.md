ADAMANT won the community voting on Atomars exchange with 10,551 votes—the highest among all competing tokens. This result secures ADM token listing on the platform.

### About Atomars

Atomars is a digital asset trading platform featuring an advanced matching system, shared liquidity, and a robot-friendly API. The exchange does not require KYC verification; account creation requires only email confirmation. Trading is fully mobile-friendly.

![ADAMANT has won the voting on Atomars exchange](/images/engineering-notes/medium/5664b587c18/002-0-tcpp1soqsq3s1dqf.webp)

At the time of listing, Atomars had been operating for approximately one year and reported around 50,000 community members and platform users. The exchange listed 100 coins across 250 trading pairs with an average daily trading volume of roughly 5,000,000 USD. It was tracked on CoinGecko, Coinpaprika, Coinranking, and Nomics among other crypto data platforms. Additional platform features included decentralized private user wallets, a quick exchange function, and community project voting.

![ADAMANT has won the voting on Atomars exchange](/images/engineering-notes/medium/5664b587c18/003-0-l-ejkjtoetmam9eh.webp)

The ADM token listing on Atomars expands ADAMANT's exchange presence, making the token available to a broader trading audience without KYC requirements.
