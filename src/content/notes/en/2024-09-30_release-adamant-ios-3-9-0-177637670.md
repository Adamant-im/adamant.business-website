This release introduces an extra layer of protection to resist blocking, making it increasingly difficult to restrict ADAMANT. Local storage encryption has been improved with two layers of encryption, both now requiring keys from a separate processor.

Public KLY messages attached to transactions are now visible within the app. Read and unread quick actions have been added to the chat list, allowing users to swipe a chat to mark it as read or unread.

The app design has been slightly updated. A few bugs have been fixed, improving overall messenger stability. In-app notifications have been customized, making the Notifications section more user-friendly and flexible.
