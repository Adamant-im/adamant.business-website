The ADAMANT Trading & Market-making bot has been updated to version 1.5.0. The release introduces dynamic order book construction on the Bit-Z and CoinDeal exchanges, allowing the bot to maintain and refresh bid/ask levels automatically as market conditions change rather than relying on statically configured orders.

The bot source code and release notes are available on the ADAMANT GitHub repository under the `adamant-tradebot` project. Installation and configuration instructions are documented alongside the project materials.
