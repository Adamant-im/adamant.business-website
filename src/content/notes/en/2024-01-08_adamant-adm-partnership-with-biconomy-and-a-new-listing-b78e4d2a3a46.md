ADAMANT has entered into a partnership with Biconomy Exchange, a global digital asset trading platform headquartered in Canada. Both projects share a compatible technological approach, which shaped the direction of the collaboration.

The partnership covers ADM blockchain integration into Biconomy's trading platform and an upcoming ADM/USDT spot listing, along with co-marketing activities. Biconomy has already completed the ADAMANT blockchain integration, though the exact listing date has not yet been announced. Biconomy's own announcement confirms the planned spot trading pair.

### About Biconomy

Biconomy is a global digital asset trading platform known for transparent trading services, with strong rankings on CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap and offices in South Korea, Japan, CIS, and the UK. The platform offers iOS and Android apps alongside a web-based trading application.

### About ADAMANT

ADAMANT is a decentralized anonymous messenger built on its own blockchain. It is independent of governments, corporations, and even its own developers, thanks to a distributed network infrastructure running open-source code. Apps are available across Web, Tor, iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and GNU/Linux.

ADM is a delegated Proof-of-Stake utility token used for message and data transfers, with a 5-second block time. Message fees sustain the decentralized messenger infrastructure. A built-in crypto exchanger allows ADM swaps for other cryptocurrencies directly within the messenger apps. Anyone can set up a network delegate to forge ADM or run a forging pool to share rewards among voters.

Beyond the messenger, the ADAMANT ecosystem includes ADAMANT Business (a private blockchain for companies), ADAMANT 2FA (a blockchain-based OTP solution), and ADAMANT TradeBot (a self-hosted market-making tool for crypto projects).
