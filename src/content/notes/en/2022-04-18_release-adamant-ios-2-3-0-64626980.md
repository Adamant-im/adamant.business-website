This release of the ADAMANT iOS app introduces support for reading ADAMANT QR codes generated by the Android and Desktop applications, improving cross-platform interoperability for users who share contacts or payment requests.

Several bug fixes are included in this version. A bug affecting static chat names has been resolved, ensuring that conversation labels display correctly. Additionally, the buy ADM row has been restored to the interface, and various other bug fixes have been applied to improve overall stability.
