This release introduces Adelina, an artificial intelligence (AI) chatbot based on ChatGPT. The Bet on Bitcoin price chat has been removed.

ADAMANT AI Chatbot is software that allows you to launch your own anonymous artificial intelligence (AI) chatbot based on ChatGPT in ADAMANT Messenger.

Some examples of using an AI chatbot include customer support, where the bot answers common customer inquiries and provides assistance with products or services. It can also serve as a personal assistant, helping users manage their schedules, set reminders, and receive personalized recommendations based on their preferences. Additional use cases include information retrieval for quick access to weather forecasts, news updates, or sports scores, as well as order management for placing orders, tracking status, and providing support throughout the process. The chatbot can also support language learning by practicing speaking and listening skills in a foreign language with instant feedback and corrections.

Beyond these scenarios, the AI chatbot can draft emails or other pieces of writing, write Python code, answer questions about a set of documents, support conversations, tutor in a range of subjects, translate languages, and simulate characters for video games, among other applications.
