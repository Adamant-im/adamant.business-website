This release introduces support for the FameEX and NonKYC exchanges, expanding the range of platforms the bot can operate on. The bot name is now automatically composed in an ADM/USDT@Coinstore-acc1 TradeBot-like format, providing clearer identification across accounts and exchanges.

Coin networks have been added, enabling conversion of exchange-specific network names into the bot's internal format. This improves compatibility when working with multiple exchanges that use differing network naming conventions. Dependencies have been updated, and several small fixes are included.
