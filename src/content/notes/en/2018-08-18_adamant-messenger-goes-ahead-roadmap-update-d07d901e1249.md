ADAMANT launched in early 2017 and has since delivered substantial progress on its privacy-focused messaging platform. The project continues to evolve through ongoing development and community engagement.

Based on user feedback gathered through meetups and community discussions, ADAMANT has revised its roadmap. The updated plan addresses several frequently requested capabilities, including group chats, deletable messages, and secure transmission of files and images. These additions aim to strengthen the platform's privacy guarantees while expanding its practical utility for everyday communication.
