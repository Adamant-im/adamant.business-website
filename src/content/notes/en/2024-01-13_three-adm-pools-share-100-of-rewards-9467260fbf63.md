ADAMANT has launched three new forging pools: donates_pool, developers_pool, and marketing_pool. Each pool distributes 100% of forging rewards to voters and pays out once a month.

None of these pools are forging yet. They need to accumulate enough votes to rank among the top 101 delegates on the ADAMANT network, which is the threshold required to actively forge blocks and generate rewards.

Voting for pools and delegates helps support decentralization of the ADAMANT blockchain. A full list of ADAMANT pools and a voting guide is available in the ADAMANT news repository.
