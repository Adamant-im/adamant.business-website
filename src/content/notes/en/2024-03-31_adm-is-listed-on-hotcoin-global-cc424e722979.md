ADM, the native cryptocurrency of the ADAMANT blockchain, has been listed on Hotcoin Global Exchange. Trading for the ADM/USDT pair opened on April 3 at 15:00 UTC+0. This listing expands ADM's liquidity and global market accessibility.

Hotcoin Global is a digital asset trading platform operating in over 97 countries, providing spot exchange, derivatives, and crypto loan services. The addition of ADM to Hotcoin Global connects the ADAMANT ecosystem with a broader base of global traders.

ADAMANT Messenger is a decentralized, blockchain-based communication platform that emphasizes user privacy and anonymity. It operates without requiring phone numbers or emails for account creation. The platform features a built-in wallet, enabling users to send and receive ADM tokens directly within the chat interface.
