Private communication and blockchain infrastructure your organization controls — messenger, identity, and automation in one engineering stack.

## For organizations that need control

ADAMANT Business is not just a blockchain — it is a messaging platform with wallets, tokens, bots, and services your IT team can run on company nodes. Data stays on infrastructure you operate. You can audit, restrict, and recover on your terms.

## Understandable for real teams

- Install and maintain with ordinary IT skills — no months of Hyperledger consultants to try a corporate chain
- Fair dPoS consensus — modest hardware, predictable costs
- Built-in multi-chain wallets with user-controlled keys
- In-chat transfers, 2FA, voting, notifications, and custom bots
- Open source since 2016 — inspect everything

## Messenger plus internal economy

Your staff already understands messengers. Add an internal token transferable in-chat for settlements, rewards, or access — without drawing enterprise diagrams for every workflow.
